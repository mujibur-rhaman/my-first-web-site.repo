Jamia Islamia Obaidia Nanupur

popularly known as Nanupur Madrasah

is a famous Qawmi Jamiah.

The Jamiah was established by Shah Sultan Ahmad Nanupuri in 1958.

It is situated at Nanupur, Fatikchhari, Bangladesh.

There are 5000 students at present in

the Jamiah receiving education in

various subjects and 80 well qualified teachers.
